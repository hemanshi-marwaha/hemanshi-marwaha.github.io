About

Hey there! I’m Hemanshi—your go-to Data Analyst with a splash of creativity and a knack for making the complex, simple.

In the world of data, I’m the person who turns piles of numbers into stories that people actually want to read. Data analysis? Check. Market intelligence? Double check. But here’s the twist: I don’t just crunch numbers—I turn them into something you can understand, whether you're a data geek or someone who still counts on their fingers. My communication and presentation skills? Top-notch. I’ve got a talent for making even the most intimidating data look like a walk in the park.

But hold on, there's more. When I'm not decoding data, I'm diving into my artistic side. I’ve been teaching math since I was a kid (seriously, it’s been that long), and I love making those light bulbs go off for others. I also sketch—because sometimes, creativity demands a pencil instead of a keyboard. When I’m not with a pencil in hand, I’m probably out trekking, camping, or lost in a good book, feeding my curiosity and love for the great outdoors.

So yeah, whether it's numbers, art, or exploring the wild, I bring a mix of creativity, clarity, and just a hint of adventure to everything I do.
